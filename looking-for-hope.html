Our Story

Resources, encouragement, and a place to begin again.

The Light in the Window is more than a book. It is the true story of loss, resilience, faith, and the life-changing impact of people who chose to leave a light on for children who had nowhere else to go.

This page is being prepared for the full narrative that follows Theodore Johnson's journey from childhood through foster care, meeting Peg and Jerry Gorman, discovering hope, and ultimately creating the Leave One Light On movement to encourage others to become a light in someone's darkest hour.

During this V1 launch, we invite you to explore the books, learn about the Gorman family, and join a growing movement built on one simple belief:

Broken people do not need perfect people. They need people who stay.
