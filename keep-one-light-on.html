A Movement of Hope

Leave One Light On

One Light. One Life. One Decision.

I was once the child searching for a light in the darkness. Someone left theirs on for me.

Jerry and Peg Gorman opened their home when they had every reason to close the door. Their example became the foundation of The Light in the Window and inspired the Leave One Light On movement.

This movement is not about perfection. It is about ordinary people making extraordinary decisions to encourage, mentor, foster, serve, and love someone who desperately needs hope.

Broken people do not need perfect people. They need people who stay.
